What is a Demat Account?
A Demat account is used to hold shares and securities in electronic form. It facilitates easy trading and transfer of shares.
How can I open a Trading Account?
To open a trading account, you need to submit your KYC documents, fill out an application form, and follow the procedures of the chosen brokerage firm.
What is a Mutual Fund?
A Mutual Fund is a type of investment vehicle that pools money from various investors to invest in securities like stocks, bonds, and other assets.
